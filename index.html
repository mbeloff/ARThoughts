Painting workshops with Maaret Webb, teaching artist and Certified Bob Ross Instructor

Bob Ross CRI®

Find freedom on this canvas. Believe that you can do it, ‘Cuz you can do it. You can do it.Bob Ross

Take part in a workshop and learn to paint in the style of the world's favourite painting instructor, with the Bob Ross Wet-on-Wet Technique®. Using this technique and official Bob Ross products and paints, you'll produce stunning and realistic landscape paintings in as little as a few hours.
